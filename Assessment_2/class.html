Classes
All our classes are taught by fluent French speakers - native speakers when possible. At Coucou it is all about quality and supporting our students on their journey to learn French. Whether a beginner or an advanced speaker we have a class that suits you. You can make your class timetable as light or as busy as you want with our flexible scheme. We offer multiple class options so you personalise your timetable to fit in with your week. We also provide support to the students who wish to take the formal exams. For those who want a one on one environment we offer private tutoring.

Adults
A1 is for people without much experience of French. This level focuses on basic grammar and vocabulary. From learning how to order a drink to how to ask for directions we have you covered.

A2 builds on the teachings of A1 and covers more complicated grammar and vocabulary. Learn how to communicate about yourself in the past, present and future tense to others and understand them in return. This is a great course if you have some experience and want survival level French.

B1 is great for those with a strong foundation in French grammar and a wide vocabulary. This class is aimed at those who can hold a conversation in French and can talk on a wide range of subjects from current events to talking about your favourite artist.

B2 is aimed for those who can understand and produce complex text on a wide range of subjects and can talk with a fluent French speaker without too much strain on either side.

Kids & Teens
We want to make learning French fun for all ages especially for our kids and teens. From preschool to high school we have you covered.

Our preschool programme teaches French through songs and stories. Our teachers focus on imagination and creativity and foster a caring and nurturing environment.

Our primary school classes are centred on fun activities and games. Rather than formal learning we focus on simply exposing the kids to the sound of French and to pass on a love of the French language.

Our high school classes are designed to help those doing NCEA and those who are simply interested in the language. Our classes are formally structured but with a strong focus on self-expression and getting to know the French culture as well as the language. We also offer a school holiday programme but be warned this fills up fast so be sure to book in plenty of time.

Adult Classes:

At Coucou we offer a flexible approach to booking classes. We charge for a set number of classes but you control how and when you take those classes. We have a custom designed booking system that allows you to custom design your class schedule. Whether you want to book upfront for the same day each week or if you want to take it week by week our system is there to make it easy. Classes fill up quick so keep an eye on the number of spaces available.

We offer the following pass options:

  • 10 classes for $200
  • 15 classes for $280
  • 20 classes for $360
  • 25 classes for $440
  • 30 classes for $520

The pass is valid for up to six months depending on the number of classes purchased. However, please note refunds are not available if you do not use all the passes by the expiry date.
