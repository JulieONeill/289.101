Classes
All our classes are taught by fluent French speakers - native speakers when possible. At Coucou it is all about quality and supporting our students on their journey to learn French. Whether a beginner or an advanced speaker we have a class that suits you. You can make your class timetable as light or as busy as you want with our flexable scheme. We offer mulitple class options so you personalise your timetable to fit in with your week. We also provide support to the students who wish to take the formal exams. For those who want a one on one environment we offer private tutoring.
