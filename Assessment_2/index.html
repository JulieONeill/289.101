Bienvenue à "Coucou"!
Welcome to the Coucou community where learning in the classroom is only one of our many opportunities on offer. Our mission is to share our love of France and the French language with you. We offer classes for all levels and ages in our purpose-built premises. Speaking French is more fun when you get to use it in real life so we run a wide range of social events. This not only gives you the opportunity to practice your French but meet other French speakers. Another fun thing we do at Coucou is organise two trips a year to different parts of France. Have fun exploring a French city without all the stress of planning and organising.
