Guided Trips
Learning a French is fun but travelling to France to speak it is even better! Coucou has partnered with a number of French tour companies to offer the unique opportunity to attend guided tours at an affordable price. We offer two trips a year and no one trip is the same. What makes our guided trips so unique is we bring the feeling of family and community we nurture at Coucou with us. We provide this opportunity because we want to share our love of France with you. Because our tours are unique we can personalise them to the groups interests. The cities we explore change from year to year and we welcome any suggestions of a good destination! If the dates picked don't suit do not disappear, our tour partners also offer discounts on their other tours. Just book through us and start planning your one-of-a-kind adventure today.
