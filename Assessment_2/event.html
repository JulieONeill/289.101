Social Events
Everything is more fun when it’s social. As well as our classic classes we love to spice things up with our wide range of social events. At Coucou we really want to create a feeling of community and family where everyone feels welcomed and included. Whether you are a beginner or advanced we welcome all levels to our events. It can be scary to actually converse in a language that isn’t your mother tongue but our events are about trying your best and doing what you can do. Speaking as much as you can and listening to those around you will help you build on the work done in class. We offer regular planned activities but also one-off events so keep an eye on our website or social media to find out about upcoming events!
